Welcome Cavs Fans!

This page is dedicated to the Cleveland Cavaliers, a team with a rich history and passionate fanbase. From the Miracle of Richfield to the 2016 NBA Championship, the Cavs have provided countless memorable moments.

Key Moments & Eras:

  1. The Early Years & Miracle of Richfield (1970s): Establishing the franchise and the iconic playoff run.
  2. The Price/Daugherty/Nance Era (Late 80s/Early 90s): A period of consistent contention.
  3. The LeBron James Era (Part 1 & 2):
    • First overall pick (2003)
    • First NBA Finals appearance (2007)
    • Return of LeBron (2014)
    • NBA Championship (2016) - A historic comeback!
  4. The Post-LeBron Rebuild & Young Core (Present): Building for the future with exciting young talent like Darius Garland, Donovan Mitchell, and Evan Mobley.
